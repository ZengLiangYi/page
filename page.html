Harmonic Collection 2024

Welcome to my Harmolic Collection!

In my Harmolic Collection, I capture the everyday moments of a semester through weekly entries detailing what I ate and did.

I really enjoy focusing on the ordinary things in life; these small things make my happiness tangible and give me a greater sense of the reality of living.

This project, more than a diary, is a reflection on my life journey and a vessel for memories. I hope through this project, you may find the beauty in the ordinary and consider the narrative potential of your daily lives, celebrating the routine as a meaningful part of experience.

In the first week of my sophomore year, I felt a bit nervous😓. After class, I passed by Fishs Eddy, a really interesting home goods store. Ever since I was little, I've always loved these kind of delicate and cute tableware. I also passed by a second-hand tableware store where a pink teapot was displayed on the sidewalk. It was incredibly cute.

At home, I felt a bit bored and started playing cards with my boyfriend. We used a deck of Crayon Shin-chan themed playing cards that we bought during our summer trip to Jeju Island. The designs on the cards are just too creative and cute. 🃏
